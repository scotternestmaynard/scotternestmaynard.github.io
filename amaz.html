Peak Hiring Campaign

As a Production Designer I helped a small creative team responsible for making assets for holiday workforce hiring. We launched a new campaign using 3D assets. I created some of the assets. The snowflakes, moon, string and rings were made by me. Other 3D assets I helped light and animate. Our team made over 4000 unique ads over the 4 month holiday hiring season where we exceeded expectations and helped hire over 100,000 people.
