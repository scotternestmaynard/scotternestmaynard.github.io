Bowl of Noodles

Redesign

Bowl Noodle rebrand for a "healthier" market. The direction for this project was geared towards a simple minimalistic design. Simple design for simple wholesome ingredients. An updated take on the classic Bowl Noodle for health conscious consumers. After several rounds of iterations the final design was narrowed in on. High contrast packaging with minimalistic qualities. The package size has been reduced in an effort to conserve paper and manufacturing costs.
